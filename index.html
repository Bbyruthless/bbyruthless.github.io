About Me

Bbyruthless aka Ruthless is a variety streamer and VTuber who has been consistently & full-time streaming since Summer 2022. She portrays the character Saint Gomez on the GTA RP server NoPixel WL. As well as GTA V, she is also known for Just Chatting streams and playing other variety games, which never fail to entertain.

When tuning in to one of Ruthless streams you will immediately notice her focus on creating a safe and welcoming environment for all & maintaining positive vibes at all times in her community. Utilising her contagious energy and witty humour, these values are always reflected onto her content.
